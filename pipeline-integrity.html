How We Ensure Pipeline Safety

Meeting Long Island’s fuel needs with innovation and safety

State-of-the-art inspections

Every pipeline undergoes thorough internal inspection at least once every five years using advanced in-line tools—also known as “smart pigs.” These tools measure wall thickness, detect corrosion, and identify issues like dents, ensuring no defect goes unnoticed.

Targeted repairs

If any concern is identified, our expert teams respond quickly. We excavate and physically inspect affected sections, making precise repairs to preserve pipeline integrity.

Built to Last Long-Term Protection

How we extend the life of our pipelines with advanced safeguards and smart prevention.

Durable coatings

All of our pipelines are coated with high-performance materials designed to resist surface wear and environmental damage.

Cathodic protection system

Our cutting-edge system uses buried anodes to release a low-voltage current that slows natural corrosion. We regularly inspect test stations and perform close interval surveys to keep this system operating flawlessly.

Our Commitment to Safety

At Northville Industries, we prioritize the safe, efficient transport of petroleum through our extensive pipeline network. With decades of experience, we take a proactive approach to protecting both the environment and the communities we serve.

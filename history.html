1917 • Founding

Origins and Evolution

Samuel Bernstein launches Northville with a horse-drawn carriage delivering coal and ice in NYC—laying the foundation for a century of energy leadership.

1950s • Expansion

Shift to Petroleum

Led by Harold Bernstein, Northville enters the petroleum industry, building major terminals and pipelines across Long Island to fuel rapid regional growth.

1979 • Panama

Expansion to Panama

Northville partners with Panama to create Petroterminal de Panama and builds vital transshipment and pipeline infrastructure connecting the Pacific and Atlantic.

2000s • Growth

Leading Forward

Under Jay and Gene Bernstein, Northville advances into trading, becomes a major player on NYMEX/CME, and integrates sustainability into its strategy.

Present • Local Impact

Community Involvement

Northville supports Long Island through charity events like the Long Island Classic and partnerships with groups like United Way, earning broad recognition.

Future • Vision

Leading with Purpose

Northville continues to lead with integrity, focusing on innovation, service, and sustainable energy to shape the future of the petroleum industry.

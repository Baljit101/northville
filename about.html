About Us

Powering Long Island with
Purpose, Progress, and Integrity For over a century, Northville Industries has served as a trusted energy partner to the communities we call home. From our humble beginnings delivering coal and ice to becoming a regional leader in fuel supply and renewable energy, our mission remains unchanged: to supply energy with integrity, reliably, responsibly, and with a vision for a cleaner tomorrow.

What drives usCore Values

At Northville Industries, our work is guided by a clear set of values that shape every decision we make, from the fuels we deliver to the communities we serve.

Integrity

We hold ourselves to the highest ethical standards, ensuring transparency and accountability in everything we do.

Innovation

We embrace new technologies and more sustainable solutions to drive progress across every sector of the energy industry.

Community

We invest in people and partnerships that uplift Long Island and ensure a stronger, more sustainable future for all.

Our CommitmentGiving Back

Northville Industries supports local nonprofits through donations that promote hunger relief, education, safety, and family wellness.

Across Panama

Our Atlantic and Pacific terminals, connected by a 131 km pipeline, power efficient, cross-country fuel transport.

Atlantic Terminal

Located in Chiriquí Grande, this terminal handles large tankers via SBM and CALM buoys.

Berthing Depth: Up to 74 feet

Pacific Terminal

Storage Capacity: 7.5 million barrels

Docking Facilities: Two docks for VLCC

About the Terminal

Petroterminal de Panamá (PTP) plays a key role in global petroleum logistics, specializing in the storage and transshipment of crude oil. Through its 131-kilometer Trans-isthmian Pipeline, PTP connects the Atlantic and Pacific coasts of Panama, enabling efficient and secure movement of hydrocarbons between major international markets.

  • International Distribution

    Connects Latin American production with global destinations via the Panama Canal.

  • Security & Compliance

    Strict adherence to safety and environmental protocols to ensure reliable operations.

  • Scalable Capacity

    Supports increasing demand with flexible storage and pumping systems.

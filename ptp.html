Our Terminals Strategic Locations
Across Panama

Our Atlantic and Pacific terminals, connected by a 131 km pipeline, power efficient, cross-country fuel transport.

Atlantic Terminal

Located in Chiriquí Grande, this terminal handles large tankers via SBM and CALM buoys.

Storage Capacity: 7 million barrels

Berthing Depth: Up to 74 feet

Pacific Terminal

Situated near Puerto Armuelles, Chiriquí, it features docks for VLCCs and extensive storage facilities.

Storage Capacity: 7.5 million barrels

Docking Facilities: Two docks for VLCC

Trans-isthmian Pipeline

A 131 km pipeline transporting crude oil between the Atlantic and Pacific terminals.

Length:131 KM

Monthly Throughput: 7 million barrels

Our Infrastructure Core Services

From large-scale storage to cross-country pipeline movement, our operations ensure secure, efficient fuel handling across Panama.

Hydrocarbons Storage

With over 14 million barrels of capacity, our terminals offer secure and efficient storage for crude oil and refined products on both the Atlantic and Pacific coasts.

Hydrocarbon Transportation

Our 131 km trans-isthmian pipeline connects the Atlantic and Pacific coasts of Panama, moving millions of barrels each month with safety and efficiency.

About the Terminal

Our operations in Petroterminal de Panamá play a pivotal role in international petroleum logistics. From refined fuels to heavy crude, the terminal supports efficient, secure, and scalable transport to global markets.

Locations

Port Jefferson Facility

Located on the waterfront, the Port Jefferson Terminal is Northville Industries’ marine receiving point for bulk fuel shipments. With a dedicated tanker berth and direct pipeline connection to inland terminals, this facility allows for safe, high-capacity offloading of product from vessels. It is the starting point of our precision-controlled pipeline system, which transports fuel to both the Setauket and Holtsville terminals, reinforcing our commitment to reliable, efficient, and environmentally responsible operations.

Setauket Tank Farm

Our Setauket Terminal is a key link in our distribution chain, supporting the secure and efficient movement of fuel products across Suffolk County. This terminal is designed for streamlined operations and quick turnaround, helping Northville Industries maintain consistent supply levels for customers across Long Island. It works in coordination with our other terminals to ensure seamless logistics and dependable fuel access year-round.

Holtsville Terminal

Our Holtsville Terminal serves as a critical hub in Northville Industries’ fuel distribution network. Strategically located to support efficient delivery across Long Island, this facility is equipped with advanced storage and pumping systems to handle high volumes of heating oil, diesel, gasoline, and biodiesel blends. The terminal plays a vital role in maintaining product integrity, safety, and reliable service for our residential, commercial, and wholesale customers.

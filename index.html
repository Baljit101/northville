Northville Premium Diesel

Our B5 premium diesel is detergent-enriched for cleaner performance, offering enhanced reliability, protection, and efficiency.

DETERGENT
  • Meets industry requirements for CUMMINS L-10 diesel injector cleanliness
  • Maintains injector spray pattern
  • Keeps injectors clean
  • Helps maintain peak engine performance
COLD FLOW (LTFT)
  • Lowers LTFT Cold Flow Results
  • Lowers fuel pour point and CFPP
  • Increases fuel flow through filters
  • Reduces waxing and gelling
LUBRICITY IMPROVER
  • Can increase service life of sensitive fuel system components
  • Improves performance on HFRR
CORROSION INHIBITOR
  • Minimizes fuel contamination with corrosion by-products
  • Protects fuel system components
  • Reduces maintenance costs
STABILIZER
  • Inhibits the formation of filter plugging gums and residue
  • Reduces maintenance costs
  • Helps maintain injector cleanliness
DEMULSIFIER
  • Prevents some of the water related problems that can occur in diesel fuel

Investing In Our Community

For 70 years Northville Industries has been keeping Long Islanders warm through its business, and we are delighted to also be helping through our philanthropy. We salute our co-supporters at Project Warmth and encourage other local businesses to join our effort.

Gene Bernstein Chairman of Northville Industries Chair of the Project Warmth Fuel Fund Committee

Island Harvest

Northville Industries proudly supports Island Harvest, Long Island’s leading hunger-relief organization. By contributing to Island Harvest’s mission, Northville helps provide food, resources, and hope to thousands of individuals and families in need across the region.

Long Island Association

Northville Industries is an active supporter of the Long Island Association (LIA), the region’s leading business organization. Through this partnership, Northville contributes to advancing economic growth, regional development, and public policy initiatives that strengthen Long Island’s business community and overall quality of life.

Nassau County Police Department Foundation

Northville Industries proudly supports the Nassau County Police Department Foundation as part of its ongoing commitment to public safety and community partnership. This contribution reflects Northville’s dedication to strengthening local institutions and helping ensure a safer, more resilient Long Island.

Energeia at Molloy University

Northville Industries supports the Energeia Partnership at Molloy University, a leadership academy focused on tackling Long Island’s most pressing social and economic challenges. Through this involvement, Northville invests in the development of ethical, informed leaders committed to creating lasting, positive change in the region.

United Way’s Project Warmth

Northville Industries is a proud supporter of United Way’s Project Warmth, a crucial emergency energy assistance program that helps Long Island families heat their homes during the winter. Through this partnership, Northville continues its mission to provide not just fuel, but comfort and care to the communities it serves.

Our International Partnerships

Petroterminal de Panamá (PTP)

Northville, through its parent company NIC Holding Corp., is a founding partner of Petroterminal de Panamá (PTP) — a key energy hub connecting the global oil market.

PTP operates a 131 km pipeline linking the Atlantic and Pacific coasts of Panama, enabling efficient storage and transfer of hydrocarbons between oceans and strengthening international energy flow.

Bioheat® Fuel

Cleaner Heating Oil for a Cleaner New York

Northville Industries delivers sustainable heating oil blends that reduce emissions and reward customers through state tax incentives.

  • Environmental Impact

    Bioheat® fuels produce fewer carbon emissions and airborne particulates compared to traditional heating oil, making them a cleaner choice for both homes and the planet.

  • State Incentives

    New York State offers clean fuel tax credits of up to $0.20 per gallon for B20 blends, making Bioheat® both eco-friendly and cost-effective.

  • Drop-In Compatibility

    Biofuel blends like B5 and B20 work seamlessly with existing oil heating systems—no modifications required.

Safer Infrastructure

From pipeline integrity to terminal logistics, our systems are built for long-term safety, efficiency, and environmental compliance.

FOR OPERATIONS

Enhancing safety through proactive infrastructure monitoring

We use advanced systems to protect our pipeline and terminal operations—prioritizing environmental safety and system longevity.

  • Real-time monitoring of pipeline pressure, flow, and corrosion
  • Automated alerts for anomalies and leak detection
  • Ongoing compliance with SPCC and FRP safety regulations
  • Preventive maintenance to reduce risk and extend infrastructure life

FOR TERMINALS

Optimizing storage and distribution with smart systems

Our fuel terminals leverage automation and data to improve efficiency, safety, and responsiveness across every step of distribution.

  • Automated fuel inventory tracking and supply management
  • Efficient scheduling and routing of tanker deliveries
  • Reduced downtime through proactive equipment servicing
  • Centralized control of terminal data and performance
